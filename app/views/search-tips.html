Get better results when you search on Find UK Government Data

Choose your search words with care

Choose words that will likely appear on the title or description of the dataset.

Use the geographical area filter

If the data you're looking affects a specific area only, use the geographical area filter to get more relevant results.

Keep the publisher in mind

If you're looking for data that spans to a national level or affects only a local authority, you can narrow down your search with the publisher filter by choosing the type of organisation that would publish that kind of data.

View the publisher page if you know them

If you know who has published the data you're looking for, you can visit their page on Find UK Government data and browse through their datasets or get in touch if you can't find what you're looking for.
